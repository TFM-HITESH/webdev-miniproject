About Me

Who Am I?

I am Dr. Ganesh Khekare, an Associate Professor at Vellore Institute of Technology, India, specializing in Computer Science & Engineering. With a Ph.D. and postdoc from esteemed institutions, along with a UGC NET qualification in Computer Science (98.15 percentile), I bring over 14 years of expertise to the table.

My research spans Data Science, AI, Machine Learning, IoT, Networking, and Blockchain, resulting in over 70 publications in top-tier journals and conferences, with 400+ citations. I hold 5 patents, 10 copyrights (including international recognition), and secured grants totaling 5.5 lakhs.

My commitment extends to academic service, including organizing international events, serving on editorial boards, and reviewing for esteemed journals. I've participated in over 50 training programs from top institutions and industries, ensuring I remain abreast of the latest technologies.

Guiding numerous research projects and mentoring five Ph.D. scholars, I am dedicated to fostering the next generation of researchers. Through my multifaceted contributions, I strive to advance the frontiers of Computer Science & Engineering.
